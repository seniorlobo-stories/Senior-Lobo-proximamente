**Toast / ToastStack** — brief confirmations and warnings. Group them in a `ToastStack`.

```jsx
<ToastStack>
  <Toast tone="success">Guardado correctamente</Toast>
  <Toast tone="info">Sincronizando cambios</Toast>
  <Toast tone="warn">Conexión inestable</Toast>
</ToastStack>
```

Tones: `success` (filled accent icon), `info` (pale icon), `warn` (warm icon). Needs the Línea SVG filters.
